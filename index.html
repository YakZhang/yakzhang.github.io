Research Interest

  • Intelligent Software Engineering: By integrating artificial intelligence technologies such as large language models and deep learning, this direction advances the automation and intelligence of software engineering tasks This area has already attracted wide attention in industry, with representative products such as Cursor and Manus demonstrating its significant application potential.
  • Multi-Agent Systems: This direction explores the mechanisms of multi-agent collaboration and studies their applications in scenarios such as end-to-end code generation and intelligent assistants. Examples such as GUI Agent and Siri, laying a practical foundation for future intelligent software development.
  • Trustworthy Large Language Models: This research focuses on the key challenges faced by large language models, such as hallucinations, security, fairness, and robustness. It explores new methods for automatically detecting and repairing defects in AI models, driving the development of more trustworthy and controllable paradigms for large language models.
